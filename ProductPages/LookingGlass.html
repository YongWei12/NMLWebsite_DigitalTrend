About the Product

LookingGlass is a standalone News Round-Up generation tool that enables news organisations to provide mobile-friendly news formats to their readers. In each News Round-Up, news stories can be curated with the aim to alleviate news fatigue for busy readers.







Besides the main spotlight article, the News Round-Up also features automatically generated past coverage from the same news organisation as well as video coverage. Global coverage from other news organisations is an optional feature that users can opt for to provide a broader array of perspectives. Users can also embed a tweet to allow topic engagement on their social media.

The LookingGlass is designed to enable easy sharing. Organisations need only upload the website URL to their respective social media platforms to enable readers' easy access to the site.
